About the song

“Fire” is a song by an English singer and songwriter Barns Courtney. The song belongs to the folk rock genre which combines the elements of both folk and rock music. I run into the song quite recently and felt in love with it almost right away. When I heard the song for the first time I was caught by the powerful, rhythmic music. After that, when listened to the piece again I paid attention to the lyrics and I found it deep and inspiring. I understand it as a call for action. In my opinion, the singer appeals to leave behind everything that is insignificant and do what you love to do.
